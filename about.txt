5. AI Rationale: Macro Context
This shows the broader financial environment the AI checked before making the trade. For example, it looks at the VIX (Fear index) and DXY (US Dollar Strength). If these metrics indicate a massive global market crash, the bot will refuse to trade, regardless of the technical indicators.

6. Live News Feed
This displays the actual headlines the bot is reading from Cointelegraph and CNBC in real-time.

VADER Score: Every article is given a score from -1.0 (extremely negative) to +1.0 (extremely positive).
Agg Sentiment: The total average mood of the market. If the AI wants to BUY Bitcoin, but the Agg Sentiment is -0.50 (highly negative), the AI's risk-management constraints will override the model and cancel the trade.
